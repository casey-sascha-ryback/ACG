How the Password Analyzer Works
AI-Driven Analysis with TensorFlow

Our password analyzer uses scikit-learn, trained on common password patterns to evaluate strength beyond simple character composition. These machine learning models recognize complex patterns and vulnerabilities, providing more nuanced analysis than traditional methods.

Privacy Focus

All analysis is performed locally in your browser and on our server. No passwords are stored, logged, or transmitted to third parties. We use state-of-the-art security practices to ensure your data remains private.

What Makes a Strong Password?
  • Length: At least 12 characters for high security
  • Complexity: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Unpredictability: Avoid common words and patterns
  • Uniqueness: Different password for each account
Pro Tip: Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
